Emerald Advisors

Pro Bono M&A and Valuation Services for Canadian Lower Middle Market Businesses

Our Mission

Emerald Advisors provides pro bono mergers and acquisitions (M&A) advisory services to lower middle-market businesses in Canada. Our team is dedicated to helping businesses grow by offering buy and sell-side advisory, valuation services, and professionally crafted marketing materials to reach a wider range of acquirers.

Our Services

Buy & Sell-Side Advisory

We assist businesses on both buy and sell-side transactions, ensuring smooth and strategic deals.

Valuation Services

Providing free, comprehensive valuation services to help businesses understand their market worth.

Marketing Materials

Creation of teasers and Confidential Information Memorandums (CIM) to help businesses reach a broader audience of acquirers.
